Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your TikTok or Instagram account, we collect:

  • Public profile information (username, display name, avatar, bio, follower count)
  • Video metadata (titles, descriptions, view counts, likes, comments, shares)
  • Engagement metrics and performance data

We do not collect private messages, passwords, or financial information.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Display analytics and performance insights within the Service
  • Generate AI-powered content recommendations
  • Track historical performance trends

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to external servers. API tokens are stored securely in local environment files.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Your social media data is only processed through official platform APIs (TikTok, Instagram) and AI services (Anthropic Claude) to provide analytics.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained locally for as long as you use the Service. You can delete all stored data at any time by removing the application files from your device.

6. Third-Party Services

The Machine integrates with:

  • TikTok API — for retrieving your video and profile data
  • Anthropic Claude API — for AI-powered analysis (your data is processed per Anthropic's privacy policy)

7. Your Rights

You can disconnect your social media accounts at any time, which revokes The Machine's access to your data. You may also request deletion of all stored data.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at privacy@themachine.app.
